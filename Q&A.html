Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Dress is semi-formal. The ceremony will be outdoors, so we recommend block heels or flats, and bringing a light jacket for the evening.

Can I bring a guest?

Due to venue capacity, we ask that only those listed on the invitation attend. Thank you for understanding!

Are kids welcome?

While we love your little ones, this will be an adults-only celebration. We hope you enjoy a night out!

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 3:30 PM. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00 PM.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes! Free parking is available at the Chesapeake Bay Foundation. Signs and attendants will help direct you upon arrival.

Where are you registered?

Your presence is our gift! But if you’d like to contribute, we’re registered at Zola.

I have a dietary restriction. Can I let you know?

Yes, please indicate any allergies or dietary needs on your RSVP form. We’ll do our best to accommodate you!
