Travel & Lodging

Recommended Airport

The closest airport to Annapolis is Baltimore/Washington International (BWI), located about 30 minutes away by car. BWI is well-connected and offers a variety of car rental and shuttle options.

Things to Do in Annapolis

  • Downtown Annapolis: Walk the charming cobblestone streets, shop local boutiques, and grab ice cream by the harbor.
  • U.S. Naval Academy: Take a guided tour of the historic campus and its stunning architecture.
  • Boating & Sailing Tours: Enjoy a sunset cruise or rent a paddleboard to explore the bay.
  • Historic Maryland State House: Visit the oldest state capitol still in continuous legislative use.
  • Local Seafood Restaurants: Try Maryland crab cakes at favorites like Chick & Ruth’s Delly or Boatyard Bar & Grill.
