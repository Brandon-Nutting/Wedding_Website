Wedding Day Schedule

3:30 PM – Guest Arrival

Please arrive at the Chesapeake Bay Foundation early to park, find your seat, and take in the beautiful views. There will be a shuttle from the Hilton Garden Inn to the venue.

4:00 PM – Ceremony

The ceremony will begin promptly on the lawn overlooking the water. Get ready for a joyful, tear-filled celebration of love!

4:30 PM – Cocktail Hour

Enjoy light bites, drinks, and music while the wedding party takes photos.

5:30 PM – Reception & Dinner

Head inside for a delicious dinner, heartfelt toasts, and dancing. We'll keep the party going all night!

9:30 PM – Send-Off

Join us outside for a sparkler send-off as we wrap up the evening with some final magic under the stars.
